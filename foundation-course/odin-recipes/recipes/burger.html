Burger

Description

A perfectly seasoned, juicy beef patty with lettuce, tomato, onion, and your choice of creamy or tangy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Chicken Patty

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add garlic if using, and saut√© briefly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated cheese until smooth.
  4. Return the drained spaghetti to the pot or a large bowl. Quickly add the pancetta (with its fat) and the egg-cheese mixture, tossing vigorously to coat the pasta. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time until a creamy sauce forms.
  5. Season with freshly ground black pepper and more cheese if desired. Serve immediately.
